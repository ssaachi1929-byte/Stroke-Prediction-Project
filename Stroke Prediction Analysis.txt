This projects helps in identifying the components of a patients help. The goal of this project is to predict if their is a risk of stroke based on gender, age, hypertension, heart disease, ever married, work type, residence type, average glucose level, BMI, and smoking status. 
There were some people who had stroke previously. I did my project on ages 50 and above. The project was started by identifying the dataset to use, and I used the Kaggle dataset, which contained 5111 rows of data with ID, gender, age, hypertension, heart disease, ever married, work type, residence type, average glucose level, BMI, smoking status, and stroke. 
I used Anaconda Navigator and used the Jupyter notebook. I used the Python language and wrote the code to clean my data. I used pandas to clean my data. 
I also removed the duplicates and filtered the data to showcase patients above 50 years of age. After downloading the updated dataset, I wanted to identify any structural or formatting errors.
